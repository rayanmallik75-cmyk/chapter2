About Design Entrance Exams

Design Entrance Examinations are conducted for admission into undergraduate and postgraduate programs in Design, Fashion, Architecture, and Creative Arts. These exams assess creativity, drawing skills, visual ability, logical reasoning, and general awareness.

National Level Design Exams

  1. NID (National Institute of Design Entrance)
  2. NIFT Entrance Exam
  3. UCEED
  4. CEED
  5. NATA

University Level Design Exams

  1. Pearl Academy Entrance Exam
  2. MIT Institute of Design Entrance
  3. Symbiosis Institute of Design Entrance
