About Law Entrance Exams

A Law Entrance Examination is a competitive test conducted for admission into undergraduate and postgraduate law programs such as LL.B, BA LL.B, BBA LL.B, and LL.M. These exams assess candidates in subjects like Legal Aptitude, Logical Reasoning, English, General Knowledge, and Current Affairs.

National Level Exams

  1. CLAT (Common Law Admission Test)
  2. AILET
  3. LSAT India
  4. SLAT
  5. MH CET Law

State Level Exams

  1. AP LAWCET
  2. TS LAWCET
  3. Karnataka State Law Entrance
  4. Bihar Law Entrance Exam

University Level Exams

  1. Jamia Law Entrance
  2. Amity Law Entrance
  3. KIIT Law Entrance
