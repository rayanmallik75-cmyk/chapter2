About JEE Advanced

JEE Advanced (Joint Entrance Examination – Advanced) is one of the toughest engineering entrance exams in India conducted by one of the Indian Institutes of Technology (IITs) every year for admission into undergraduate programs in IITs.

Admission to

  • Indian Institutes of Technology (IITs)
  • Indian Institute of Science (IISc)
  • Indian Institutes of Science Education and Research (IISERs)
  • Indian Institute of Space Science and Technology (IIST)
  • Rajiv Gandhi Institute of Petroleum Technology (RGIPT)

Courses Through JEE Advanced

  • Computer Science Engineering (CSE)
  • Electrical Engineering (EE)
  • Mechanical Engineering (ME)
  • Civil Engineering (CE)
  • Chemical Engineering
  • Aerospace Engineering
  • Engineering Physics
  • Artificial Intelligence
  • ETC

Seat Distribution

  • All India Rank Based Admission
  • Category Reservation System
  • Seat allocation through JoSAA Counselling

Counselling is conducted by:

  • Joint Seat Allocation Authority (JoSAA)

JEE Advanced Exam Pattern

Subject Marks
Physics Variable
Chemistry Variable
Mathematics Variable
Total Varies every year

Marking Scheme

Type Marks
Correct Answer +3 / +4
Wrong Answer -1 / -2
Unattempted 0

Eligibility Criteria

  • Must qualify JEE Main and be among top rank holders
  • Only top 2.5 lakh candidates of JEE Main can appear
  • Maximum 2 attempts in two consecutive years
  • Must have passed 12th with Physics, Chemistry & Mathematics

Cut Off (Approx Idea)

Cut-off varies every year based on difficulty level and competition.

  • General Category: ~35%
  • OBC/EWS: ~31%
  • SC: ~17%
  • ST: ~17%

Competition Level

  • Only top 2.5 lakh students from JEE Main can appear.
  • One of the toughest exams in the world.
  • Admission into prestigious IIT institutes.
