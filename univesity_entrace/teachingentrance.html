About Teaching Entrance Exams

Teaching Entrance Examinations are conducted for admission into teacher education programs such as B.Ed, D.El.Ed, Integrated B.A/B.Sc B.Ed, and M.Ed. These exams evaluate candidates in Teaching Aptitude, Reasoning, General Knowledge, and Subject Knowledge.
