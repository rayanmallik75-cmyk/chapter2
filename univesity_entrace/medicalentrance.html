About Medical Entrance Exams

A Medical Entrance Examination is a competitive test conducted for admission into undergraduate and postgraduate medical courses such as MBBS, BDS, BAMS, BHMS, MD, MS, and allied health science programs.

Undergraduate Medical Exams

  1. NEET UG (MBBS / BDS / AYUSH)
  2. AIIMS Nursing Entrance Exam
  3. JIPMER Nursing Entrance Exam
  4. AFMC Entrance Exam

Postgraduate Medical Exams

  1. NEET PG
  2. NEET MDS
  3. INI CET
  4. NEET SS

Foreign Medical Graduate Exam

  1. FMGE (Foreign Medical Graduate Examination)
