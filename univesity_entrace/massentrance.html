About Arts & Mass Communication Exams

Arts and Mass Communication Entrance Exams are conducted for admission into undergraduate and postgraduate programs in Journalism, Media Studies, Film Making, Literature, Performing Arts, and Communication Studies. These exams assess creativity, writing skills, communication ability, general awareness, and analytical thinking.

National Level Exams

  1. CUET
  2. IIMC Entrance Exam
  3. FTII JET
  4. IPU CET (BJMC)

University Level Exams

  1. JMI Entrance Exam
  2. BHU Entrance Test
  3. XIC OET
