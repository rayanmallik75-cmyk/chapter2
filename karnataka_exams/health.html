About Health Department Exams

Karnataka Government conducts various health service recruitment examinations through KPSC, National Health Mission (NHM), and Health & Family Welfare Department.

Exams Conducted

  1. KPSC Medical Officer Recruitment Examination
  2. KPSC Specialist Medical Officer Examination
  3. KPSC Junior Health Inspector Examination
  4. KPSC Health Officer Examination
  5. KPSC Staff Nurse Recruitment Examination
  6. KPSC Pharmacist Recruitment Examination
  7. KPSC Laboratory Technician Examination
  8. KPSC Dental Health Officer Examination
  9. KPSC AYUSH Medical Officer Examination
  10. NHM Karnataka Recruitment Examination
  11. DHFWS Health Recruitment Examination
  12. Karnataka Paramedical Staff Recruitment Examination
