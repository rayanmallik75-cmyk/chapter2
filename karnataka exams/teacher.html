About Teaching Recruitment

Karnataka Government conducts various recruitment examinations for teachers in Primary Schools, High Schools, PU Colleges, Government Degree Colleges and Technical Institutions through different education departments and eligibility tests.

Teaching Exams Conducted

  1. Karnataka Teacher Eligibility Test (KARTET)
  2. Graduate Primary School Teacher (GPSTR)
  3. Primary School Teacher Recruitment
  4. High School Teacher Recruitment
  5. PU College Lecturer Recruitment
  6. Assistant Professor Recruitment
  7. Karnataka State Eligibility Test (KSET)
  8. Polytechnic Lecturer Recruitment
  9. Government ITI Instructor
  10. Physical Education Teacher (PET)
  11. Drawing / Art Teacher
  12. Special Education Teacher
