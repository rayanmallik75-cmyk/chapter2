About Karnataka Police

The Karnataka State Police (KSP) conducts recruitment examinations for various police posts such as Sub Inspector, Constable, Armed Police and Technical positions to maintain law and order across Karnataka State.

Exams Conducted

  1. Police Sub Inspector (PSI - Civil)
  2. Armed Police Sub Inspector (APSI)
  3. Reserve Sub Inspector (RSI)
  4. KSISF Sub Inspector
  5. Civil Police Constable (CPC)
  6. Armed Police Constable (APC)
  7. KSRP Police Constable
  8. KSISF Police Constable
  9. Police Bandsmen
  10. Scene of Crime Officer (SOCO)
  11. Technical & Cyber Police Posts
  12. Departmental Promotion Exams
