About KPSC

The Karnataka Public Service Commission (KPSC) conducts recruitment examinations for various Group A, Group B and Group C posts in Karnataka State Government departments including administrative, technical, police and departmental services.

Exams Conducted

  1. KAS – Gazetted Probationers
  2. First Division Assistant (FDA)
  3. Second Division Assistant (SDA)
  4. Police Sub Inspector (PSI)
  5. Assistant Engineer (AE)
  6. Junior Engineer (JE)
  7. Group C Non-Technical Posts
  8. Accounts Officer
  9. Agriculture Officer
  10. Medical Officer
  11. Excise Sub Inspector
  12. Departmental Exams
