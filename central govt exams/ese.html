About UPSC ESE

The Engineering Services Examination (ESE), also known as IES, is conducted by the Union Public Service Commission (UPSC) to recruit engineers for various technical and managerial positions in the Government of India. It is one of the most prestigious exams for engineering graduates.

Services Offered

  • Indian Railway Service of Engineers (IRSE)
  • Indian Railway Service of Mechanical Engineers (IRSME)
  • Indian Railway Service of Electrical Engineers (IRSEE)
  • Indian Railway Stores Service (IRSS)
  • Indian Defence Service of Engineers (IDSE)
  • Central Engineering Service (CPWD)
  • Military Engineering Services (MES)
  • Central Water Engineering Service
  • Central Power Engineering Service (CPES)
  • Other Central Government Technical Services

Exam Stages

  • Preliminary Examination – Objective Type (MCQs)
  • Main Examination – Conventional (Descriptive)
  • Interview – Personality Test

Marks Distribution

Stage Marks
Preliminary Examination 500
Main Examination 600
Interview 200
Total 1300

Eligibility Criteria

  • Educational Qualification: B.E./B.Tech degree in relevant engineering branch (Civil, Mechanical, Electrical, Electronics & Telecommunication)
  • Minimum Age: 21 years
  • Maximum Age: 30 years (General Category)
  • Age relaxation applicable for OBC, SC/ST and other reserved categories as per Government rules
  • Candidate must be a citizen of India

Number of Attempts

  • No fixed limit on attempts
  • Candidates can apply until they reach the maximum age limit
  • Age relaxation available for reserved categories as per Government rules

Salary

  • Starting Basic Pay: ₹56,100 per month (Level 10, 7th Pay Commission)
  • Additional allowances: DA, HRA, Transport Allowance, etc.
  • Gross salary may range between ₹70,000 – ₹1,00,000 per month (approx.)
