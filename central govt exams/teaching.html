About Teaching Exams

Central Government teaching examinations are conducted to recruit teachers and lecturers in Kendriya Vidyalayas, Navodaya Vidyalayas, Central Universities and other central educational institutions. These exams provide opportunities for candidates to work as teachers in government schools and colleges across India.

Exams Conducted

  • CTET (Central Teacher Eligibility Test)
  • KVS Recruitment (Kendriya Vidyalaya Sangathan)
  • NVS Recruitment (Navodaya Vidyalaya Samiti)
  • UGC NET (Assistant Professor / JRF)
  • CSIR NET
  • EMRS Teaching Recruitment
  • Army Public School (AWES)
  • Central University Teaching Recruitment
