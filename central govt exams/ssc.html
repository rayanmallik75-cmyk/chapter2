About SSC

The Staff Selection Commission is an important organ of the government of India that deals with the selection and recruitment of staff for various posts under the Central Government. The exam is organized to select suitable candidates who can work under different government Ministries and other departments under the central government. All exams conducted under the banner of SSC are conducted nationwide, with specific criteria for selection outlined by the government.

Exams Conducted

  • SSC CGL (Combined Graduate Level)
  • SSC CHSL (10+2 Level)
  • SSC MTS (Multi-Tasking Staff)
  • SSC CPO (Central Police Org.)
  • SSC JE (Junior Engineer)
  • SSC GD (General Duty Constable)
  • SSC Stenographer (Grade C & D)
  • SSC Junior Hindi Translator
