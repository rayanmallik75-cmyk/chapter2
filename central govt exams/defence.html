About Defence Exams

Defence examinations in India are conducted to recruit candidates into the Indian Army, Navy and Air Force. These exams provide opportunities for candidates to serve the nation as officers and soldiers in various defence services.

Exams Conducted

  • NDA (National Defence Academy)
  • CDS (Combined Defence Services)
  • AFCAT (Air Force Common Admission Test)
  • Agniveer Recruitment (Army, Navy, Air Force)
  • Indian Army Soldier Recruitment
  • Indian Navy Sailor Recruitment
  • Indian Air Force Airmen Recruitment
  • Territorial Army Officer Exam
  • Indian Coast Guard Recruitment
  • CAPF (Assistant Commandant)
