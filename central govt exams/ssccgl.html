About SSC CGL

The SSC Combined Graduate Level (CGL) Examination is conducted by the Staff Selection Commission (SSC) to recruit candidates for various Group B and Group C posts in Ministries, Departments, and Organizations of the Government of India.

Services Offered

  • Income Tax Inspector
  • Assistant Section Officer (ASO)
  • Central Excise Inspector
  • Preventive Officer
  • Auditor
  • Accountant / Junior Accountant
  • Sub Inspector (CBI & NIA)
  • Tax Assistant

Exam Stages

  • Tier I – Computer Based Test
  • Tier II – Computer Based Test
  • Document Verification

Marks Distribution

Stage Marks
Tier I Examination 200
Tier II Examination 390
Document Verification Qualifying
Total 590

Eligibility Criteria

  • Minimum Age: 18–20 years (Post Wise)
  • Maximum Age: 30–32 years (Post Wise)
  • Bachelor’s Degree from Recognized University
