About Engineering Entrance Exams

An Engineering Entrance Examination is a competitive test conducted for admission into B.E. and B.Tech programs across India. These exams evaluate students in Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics to ensure merit-based and transparent admissions.

National Level Exams

  1. JEE Main
  2. JEE Advanced
  3. BITSAT
  4. VITEEE
  5. SRMJEEE
  6. COMEDK UGET

State Level Exams

  1. KCET (Karnataka)
  2. MHT CET (Maharashtra)
  3. WBJEE (West Bengal)
  4. TS EAMCET (Telangana)
  5. AP EAMCET (Andhra Pradesh)
  6. GUJCET (Gujarat)
  7. OJEE (Odisha)

University Level Exams

  1. Manipal Entrance Test (MET)
  2. KIITEE
