About Defence Entrance Exams

Defence Entrance Examinations are conducted for admission into prestigious military academies and officer training programs in the Indian Armed Forces. These exams assess candidates in Mathematics, General Ability, Reasoning, English, and Physical Fitness.

National Level Defence Exams

  1. NDA (National Defence Academy)
  2. CDS (Combined Defence Services)
  3. AFCAT (Air Force Common Admission Test)
  4. INET (Indian Navy Entrance Test)
  5. TES (Technical Entry Scheme 10+2)

Special Defence Entry Programs

  1. Indian Naval Academy Entry
  2. Military Nursing Service Entrance
  3. Indian Coast Guard Assistant Commandant
