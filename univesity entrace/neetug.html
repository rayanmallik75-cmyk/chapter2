About NEET UG

NEET UG (National Eligibility cum Entrance Test – Undergraduate) is a national-level entrance examination in India conducted by the National Testing Agency (NTA) for admission to undergraduate medical courses like MBBS, BDS, and other allied medical programs in government and private colleges across the country..

Courses Through NEET UG

Admission to:

  • Government Medical Colleges
  • Private Medical Colleges
  • AIIMS
  • JIPMER
  • Deemed Universities

Seat Distribution

  • 15% All India Quota (AIQ)
  • 85% State Quota

Counselling is conducted by:

  • Medical Counselling Committee (MCC) – for AIQ
  • State authorities – for State quota

NEET UG Exam Pattern

Subject No. of Questions Marks
Physics 50 180
Chemistry 50 180
Biology (Botany + Zoology) 100 360
Total 200 (180 to be attempted) 720

Marking Scheme

Type Marks
Correct Answer +4
Wrong Answer -1
Unattempted 0

Eligibility Criteria

  • Minimum age: 17 years
  • Must have passed 12th (PCB – Physics, Chemistry, Biology)
  • Minimum marks:
    General: 50%
    OBC/SC/ST: 40%
    PwD: 45%

Cutt Off (Approx Idea)

Cut-off changes every year depending on:

  • Difficulty level
  • Number of candidates
  • Number of seats

Usually:

  • General category qualifying percentile: 50th percentile
  • OBC/SC/ST: 40th percentile

Competition Level

  • Around 20+ lakh students appear every year.
  • Highly competitive exam.
