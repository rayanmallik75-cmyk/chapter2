About Management Entrance Exams

A Management Entrance Examination is a competitive test conducted for admission into MBA and other postgraduate management programs. These exams assess candidates in Quantitative Aptitude, Logical Reasoning, Data Interpretation, Verbal Ability, and General Awareness.
