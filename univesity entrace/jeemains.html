About JEE Main

JEE Main (Joint Entrance Examination – Main) is a national-level engineering entrance examination conducted by the National Testing Agency (NTA) for admission into undergraduate engineering programs such as B.E., B.Tech, B.Arch, and B.Planning in NITs, IIITs, and other centrally funded technical institutions.

Admission to

  • National Institutes of Technology (NITs)
  • Indian Institutes of Information Technology (IIITs)
  • Centrally Funded Technical Institutions (CFTIs)
  • State Engineering Colleges
  • Eligibility for JEE Advanced (IIT Admission)

Courses Through JEE Main

  • Computer Science Engineering (CSE)
  • Information Technology (IT)
  • Electronics and Communication Engineering (ECE)
  • Mechanical Engineering (ME)
  • Civil Engineering (CE)
  • Electrical Engineering (EE)
  • Artificial Intelligence & Data Science
  • Chemical Engineering
  • ETC

Seat Distribution

  • All India Rank Based Admission
  • Home State Quota
  • Other State Quota

Counselling is conducted by:

  • Joint Seat Allocation Authority (JoSAA)
  • State Counselling Authorities

JEE Main Exam Pattern

Subject No. of Questions Marks
Physics 25 100

Marking Scheme

Type Marks
Correct Answer +4
Wrong Answer -1
Unattempted 0

Eligibility Criteria

  • No Minimum Age Limit
  • Must have passed 12th with Physics, Chemistry & Mathematics
  • Students can attempt JEE Main for 3 consecutive years

Cut Off (Approx Idea)

Cut-off varies every year based on difficulty level and competition.

  • General Category: ~90 Percentile
  • OBC/EWS: ~75 Percentile
  • SC: ~55 Percentile
  • ST: ~45 Percentile

Competition Level

  • More than 10 lakh students appear every year.
  • Gateway exam for IITs, NITs & IIITs.
