About UPSC IFoS

The Indian Forest Service (IFoS) Examination is conducted by the Union Public Service Commission (UPSC) to recruit officers responsible for management of forests, wildlife conservation, and environmental protection in India.

Posts in Indian Forest Service (IFoS)

  • Assistant Conservator of Forests (ACF)
  • Divisional Forest Officer (DFO)
  • Deputy Conservator of Forests (DCF)
  • Conservator of Forests (CF)
  • Chief Conservator of Forests (CCF)
  • Additional Principal Chief Conservator of Forests (APCCF)
  • Principal Chief Conservator of Forests (PCCF)

Exam Stages

  • Preliminary Exam – Conducted along with UPSC CSE
  • Mains Exam – Descriptive (Science Based)
  • Interview – Personality Test

Marks Distribution

Stage Marks
Preliminary Examination Qualifying Only
Main Examination 1400
Interview 300
Total 1700

Eligibility Criteria

  • Minimum Age: 21 years
  • Maximum Age: 32 years (General Category)
  • Degree Required in Science/Engineering/Agriculture/Forestry

Number of Attempts

Category Attempts
General 6
OBC 9
SC/ST Unlimited (Till Age Limit)

Salary

Starting Basic Pay: ₹56,100 per month + Forest Allowances + Government Facilities
