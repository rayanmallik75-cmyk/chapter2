About SSC CHSL

The SSC Combined Higher Secondary Level (CHSL) Examination is conducted by the Staff Selection Commission to recruit candidates for various clerical and administrative posts in Central Government departments for 12th pass candidates.

Services Offered

  • Lower Division Clerk (LDC)
  • Junior Secretariat Assistant (JSA)
  • Data Entry Operator (DEO)
  • Postal Assistant (PA)
  • Sorting Assistant (SA)

Exam Stages

  • Tier I – Computer Based Examination
  • Tier II – Descriptive & Skill Test

Marks Distribution

Stage Marks
Tier I Examination 200
Tier II Examination 100
Skill / Typing Test Qualifying
Total 300

Eligibility Criteria

  • Minimum Age: 18 years
  • Maximum Age: 27 years
  • Must Pass 12th Standard from Recognized Board
