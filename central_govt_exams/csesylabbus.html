UPSC CSE Syllabus

Preliminary Examination

  • Paper I – General Studies
    • Current Events of National & International Importance
    • History of India & Freedom Struggle
    • Indian & World Geography
    • Indian Polity & Governance
    • Economic & Social Development
    • Environmental Ecology & Biodiversity
    • General Science
  • Paper II – CSAT
    • Comprehension
    • Logical Reasoning
    • Basic Numeracy
    • Decision Making

Mains Examination

  • Essay Paper
  • General Studies I – History, Culture & Geography
  • General Studies II – Polity & Governance
  • General Studies III – Economy, Science & Environment
  • General Studies IV – Ethics & Integrity
  • Optional Subject (2 Papers)

Interview (Personality Test)

  • Assessment of Personality
  • Leadership & Decision Making Skills
  • Communication Skills
  • Awareness of Current Affairs
