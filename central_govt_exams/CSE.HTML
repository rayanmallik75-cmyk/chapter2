About UPSC CSE

The UPSC Civil Services Examination (CSE) is conducted annually by the Union Public Service Commission (UPSC) to recruit officers for prestigious services like IAS, IPS, IFS, and IRS. It is one of the most competitive and respected examinations in India.

Services Offered

  • Indian Administrative Service (IAS)
  • Indian Police Service (IPS)
  • Indian Foreign Service (IFS)
  • Indian Revenue Service (IRS)
  • Other Group A & B Services

Exam Stages

  • Preliminary Exam – Objective (MCQs)
  • Mains Exam – Descriptive
  • Interview – Personality Test

Eligibility Criteria

  • Minimum Age: 21 years
