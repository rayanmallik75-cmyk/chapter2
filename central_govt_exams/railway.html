About Railway Exams

Indian Railways conducts recruitment examinations through the Railway Recruitment Board (RRB) and Railway Recruitment Cell (RRC) to select candidates for various technical, non-technical and security posts. These exams provide job opportunities for candidates from 10th pass, 12th pass, ITI, Diploma and Graduate levels across India.

Exams Conducted

  • RRB NTPC (Non-Technical Popular Categories)
  • RRB Group D (Level-1 Posts)
  • RRB ALP (Assistant Loco Pilot)
  • RRB Technician
  • RRB JE (Junior Engineer)
  • RRB SSE (Senior Section Engineer)
  • RPF Sub Inspector
  • RPF Constable
  • RRB Paramedical Staff
  • RRB Ministerial & Isolated Categories
  • Railway Apprentice Recruitment
